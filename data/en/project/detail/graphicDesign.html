This is a promotional project for "Gangnam District Middle-Aged and Senior Expert Recruitment", aimed at helping with expert matching.

The illustration features a figure with an expert-like image, using blue, a color that evokes trust and reliability, to create a sense of dependability.

For the unselected design, I created an illustration of an expert in a cute style to convey a more approachable and light-hearted feeling, rather than a strictly "professional" one. I chose pink and a handwritten-style title to enhance that same friendly and welcoming vibe.

This is a campaign and poster design for the youth counseling system "Dadulgeulgae(I will listen to you all)", created using five dog characters.

The keyword "listen" was chosen, and the dog character was depicted in a listening pose, wearing a counselor's headset. Since the design style preferred was not a static or rigid character, I redrew it with a more uneven, quirky feel. However, the final design chosen was the original, neatly drawn character.
