A platform that crawls listings of used phones from various websites to compare prices. It provides not only the average prices for each phone model and manufacturer but also allows users to view stores where they can purchase used phones on a map.

The site enables direct buying and selling through the platform. This is a redesign project where the site was optimized for mobile, transitioning from a standard responsive design, greatly improving user experience.
