Expense
Tracker

The main service is price comparison for frequently purchased items.

  • Role

    Design
    (Contribution 100%)

  • Tools Used

    XD

The app consists of a ''Dashboard'' that displays the remaining amount divided into cash and card with a graph and recent transaction details, a ''List'' to view transaction history, and a 'Wallet' menu where users can set a monthly spending limit to control expenses.

This application features the ability to inform users of the lowest price and the store for frequently purchased items per specific unit, as well as a money management function that tracks expenses by decreasing from a set budget.
