A hobby recommendation website I created for a friend who was job hunting. This site, prepared as a Christmas gift, suggests activities that can help refresh and energize someone during the challenging moments of job searching.

I designed it with refreshing colors and icons, using a tangerine as the character for the website. The friendly and warm design was intended to comfort the user's heart.
