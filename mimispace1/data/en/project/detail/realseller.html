A company introduction website for RealSeller. This project involved redesigning the existing company website, splitting it into separate sections for service introduction and company information.

RealSeller offers a unique identity verification service that certifies trusted sellers. To save time, a template using CSS utility classes was purchased and used for the design, focusing on trustworthy design and clear information delivery.
