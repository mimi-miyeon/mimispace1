A recipe website with the concept of making it easy to cook healthy meals. This project, done in a team of two during a government-supported course, offers various features such as ordering ingredients, checking the fridge, and managing personal meal plans.

It was designed with large fonts to ensure that the instructions are easy to read even while cooking. Applied user experience-focused intuitive interface and responsive design to make it convenient for both mobile and desktop use.
